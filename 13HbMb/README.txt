Binding curves of two macromolecules for oxygen.

hemoglobinmyoglobin.mat: 
	- hemoglobin:
		Column 1:	concentration in M
		Column 2:	probability to be bound
	- myoglobin:
		Column 1:	concentration in M
		Column 2:	probability to be bound

hemoglobin.csv, myoglobin.csv:
	Same data in plain text file.

hemoglobin.npy, and myoglobin.npy:
	Same data in NumPy arrays.

hemoglobinmyoglobin.npz:
	Same data as hemoglobinmyoglobin.mat.


Data is taken from two articles:

	F.C. Mills, M.L. Johnson, and G.K. Ackers. 1976 Oxygenation-linked subunit
	interactions in human hemoglobin. Biochemistry 15:5350--5362

	A. Rossi-Fanelli and E. Antonini. 1958. Studies on the oxygen and carbon
	monoxide equilibria of human myoglobin. Arch Biochem Biophys 77:478--492.)
