`resting-cell.csv` is the trace for the cell in its resting state.
`exposed-cell.csv` is the trace for the cell after exposure to acetylcholine.

Katz and Miledi report, "During ACh action, an average depolarization
of 8.5 mV (from 93 to 84.5 mV) was registered." The oscilloscope traces in the
figure do not give the absolute voltage, so the voltage data have been centered
on these values.

Time values for the samples were inferred from the 50 ms scale bar in Figure 1.
