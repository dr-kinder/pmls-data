HIV infection time course. Files HIVseries.mat , HIVseries.csv, HIVseries.npy contain variable "a" with two columns of data. The first is the time in days since administration of a treatment to an HIV positive patient; the second contains the concentration of virus in that patient’s blood in arbitrary units. [Data from A Perelson. Modelling viral and immune system dynamics. Nature Revs. Immunol. (2002) vol. 2 (1) pp. 28–36 (Box 1).]
